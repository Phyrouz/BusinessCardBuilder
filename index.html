Help & Guide

Getting Started

  1. Go to Photo Library and upload your photos
  2. Go to Designer, pick a template from the left
  3. Click photo slots on the right to assign your photos
  4. Edit your info (name, measurements, contact)
  5. Click Export PDF

Designer

Templates: 50 templates in the left panel. Click any to apply.
Photos: Click any photo slot to upload an image. Use Crop/Fit toggle on each slot.
Colors: Customize text, muted, background, and accent colors.
Preview: Front and back update live.

Photo Library

Upload all your portfolio photos here. Click any photo to assign it to the next empty slot in the Designer. Photos are saved in your browser.

My Cards

Save designs by name. Click Open to reload, Delete to remove. Designs persist in your browser.

Export

Exports a print-ready PDF at 8.5 x 5.5 inches (standard comp card). Front and back on separate pages.

FAQ

Q: What size? 8.5 x 5.5 in (half letter).
Q: How many photos? 5 ideal: headshot, full body, 3 poses.
Q: Offline? Yes, standalone HTML file.
Q: My photos safe? Stored in browser localStorage.
